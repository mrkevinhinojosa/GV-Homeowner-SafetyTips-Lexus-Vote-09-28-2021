Earlier this month, Granville Homes hosted a safety meeting for our Copper River Ranch community, led by Fresno Police Department Crime Prevention Specialist SCSO Ruben Barrios. While Officer Barrios’ presentation focused on the Northeast Fresno area, he had some great suggestions for preventing theft that we would like to share with homeowners in all of our communities:

 

  1. Remove garage door release rope
  2. Add privacy film to the windows of your garage
  3. Lock all doors, including side-entry doors to the garage
  4. If you’re parked in your driveway, make sure to lock your doors and don’t leave visible items in your car
  5. Lock side gates. You may want to consider adding a code lock if you have gardeners, pest control, or others who may need to access your yard
  6. Tie your alarm system to your garage door if possible
  7. Add a motion-activated camera to the garage
  8. Landscape with hostile landscaping in front of windows. Some suggestions include holly bushes, agaves, and aromatics like rosemary
  9. Don’t hesitate to call the police to report any suspicious activity in or around your neighbors’ homes

 

These safety tips are especially important to keep in mind as the holiday season draws near. Thank you for staying vigilant and helping to keep your neighborhood safe and sound!
